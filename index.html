Chicken

Chicken is a versatile and popular choice on our menu, offering a variety of delicious and nutritious options to satisfy every palate. From our succulent grilled chicken breasts, seasoned to perfection with a blend of herbs and spices, to our crispy and flavorful fried chicken, we ensure each dish is cooked to tender, juicy perfection. Start with appetizers like crispy chicken wings tossed in a variety of sauces or savory chicken satay skewers with peanut sauce. For a light option, try our fresh chicken Caesar salad or the vibrant Thai chicken salad. Main courses feature classics such as grilled chicken breast seasoned with herbs, creamy chicken Alfredo pasta, and the flavorful butter chicken served with naan. Sandwich and wrap lovers will enjoy our chicken club sandwich and spicy buffalo chicken wrap. Explore international flavors with dishes like chicken tikka masala and jerk chicken. Beef

Our menu features a variety of beef dishes that are sure to satisfy any meat lover's cravings. From our tender, juicy steaks, cooked to your preferred level doneness, to our mouthwatering beef burgers, topped with a selection of gourmet ingredients, every bite is a testament to quality and flavor. Our slow-cooked beef brisket is a crowd favorite, marinated with a rich blend of spices and cooked until it melts in your mouth. Our beef menu caters to meat lovers with a variety of delectable dishes. Start with appetizers like tender beef skewers marinated in savory spices or classic beef sliders with gourmet toppings. For a hearty option, our beef stew is rich and comforting, simmered with root vegetables and herbs. Main courses include juicy, perfectly cooked steaks available in ribeye, filet mignon, and T-bone cuts, accompanied by your choice of sauces and sides. Our signature beef burgers feature premium patties, artisanal buns, and a range of toppings, from classic cheddar and bacon to avocado and blue cheese. For an international flair, try our beef tacos with tangy salsa, Korean beef bulgogi, or a traditional Italian beef lasagna. Complement your meal with sides like garlic mashed potatoes, sautéed mushrooms, and roasted Brussels sprouts.
